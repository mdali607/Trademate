About Us

TradeMate is an online platform designed to bridge the gap between skilled trade professionals and customers looking for reliable service providers. Founded in 2024, our mission is to provide a user-friendly and trustworthy platform where customers can connect with verified tradespeople across various fields, including plumbing, electrical work, carpentry, and more.

Our team is committed to making the booking process easy and secure, offering in-depth profiles, customer reviews, and transparent pricing information to help users make informed decisions.
